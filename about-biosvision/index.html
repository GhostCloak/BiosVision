About BiosVision

BiosVision is a clean, modern blog theme designed to put the focus on both your content and identity. It features a large sidebar photo to showcase your identity and then gets out of the way.

  • Disqus Comment Integration with manual loading for faster pages
  • Fully Responsive Embedded Videos (YouTube, Vimeo, Kickstarter) Social Network links to connect with your readers
  • Based on Bootstrap to provide a clean, easy to modify framework
  • Released under the MIT license, just like Ghost.

Support

I will be updating this theme as-needed for as long as possible and will provide general support on getting it setup. I'm unable to guarantee 'lifetime' support or anything of the sort currently however I'll do the best I can. I don't believe in promising what you can't deliver.

Future Plans

Assuming there is interest, I'm thinking of continuing to add features such as tabular sidebar content, image gallery support (eg. smugmug, g+, facebook) and several more useful features.

Why Ghost?

Ghost is a great new publishing platform that offers a simple, much-improved system for writing online. Rather than repeat them, I'll let the features do the talking. - https://ghost.org/features/

I think it's a great project and so My efforts are going towards making Ghost a viable platform by building the community and services to support it.

Why use pay-what-you-want?

It's something of an experiment. I'm an open-source kinda guy and I don't really like restricting who can access it, but I still need to eat. If you like this theme and want me to continue work on it and similar projects, please chip in whatever you think it's worth.
